Purnendu

Design. Physics. Cognition

I am an interaction designer, physicist and human cognition enthusiast. At present I am PhD student at the interdisciplinary ATLAS Institute at University of Colorado Boulder working with Daniel Leithinger, Mark D. Gross and Carson Bruns . My research lies at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction and material science. I am developing tools to facilitate interactive fabrication and shape changing interfaces. I am also interested in tools to augment human cognition.

Earlier, I co-founded a nanotechnology company in India Log 9 Materials , (which focuses on manufacturing and developing commercial applications around the wonder material, Graphene) and a multidisciplinary Design Studio at IIT Roorkee. I hold a Integrated Master's degree in physics (with specialization in material science) from the Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee. You can find my research projects below.
